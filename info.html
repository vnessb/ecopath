Global Carbon Emissions

The average global carbon footprint is approximately 4.8 metric tons per person per year, with significant variation by country.

The Corporate Issue

While individuals can make an impact, over 70% of global carbon emissions come from just 100 major companies. Systemic changes, such as regulations and corporate accountability, are crucial.

What You Can Do

Advocate for corporate responsibility, support climate policies, and take action in your own life.
